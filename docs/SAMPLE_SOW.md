# Statement of Work — Project Helios Customer Portal

**Client:** Helios Retail Group
**Vendor:** Werk Consulting
**Status:** Signed

## 1. Background

Helios Retail Group is launching a customer-facing self-service portal to replace its legacy
call-centre workflows. This SOW covers the design, build, testing, and rollout of the portal.

## 2. Scope

The engagement will deliver a customer-facing web application with a polished, responsive UI and
a supporting REST API backed by a PostgreSQL database. The team will implement account management,
order history, and a returns workflow. The solution must integrate with the existing billing
system.

## 3. Delivery approach

Delivery will follow a **hybrid** approach: an up-front architecture and requirements phase
followed by iterative agile development sprints.

## 4. Releases and rollout

The programme is planned across **3 releases**. The portal will roll out to **2 countries**:
the **United States** and the **United Kingdom**, with localized content for each market.

## 5. Quality

The QA strategy includes **5 test cycles** covering functional, integration, regression, and
user-acceptance testing before go-live.

## 6. Commercials and timeline

The total contract budget is **750000 USD** over a **12 months** duration.

## 7. Compliance

The solution must meet **GDPR** and **SOC2** compliance requirements, including data-residency
controls per market.

## 8. Acceptance

Each release is accepted on successful completion of its test cycles and sign-off by the Helios
product owner.
